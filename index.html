Their are many types of epilepsy all with different symptoms and effects. Not all forms of the conditon come with having full seizures, some forms of the conditon only give the victim partial seizures. THis means that the person will black out and become abcent for a varing amount of time.

partial seizures can be as equaly dangerouse as normal seizuresand it is coommon for the person to begin to wonder and faces the risk of getting lost or being in a fatel or near fatel acident.

Medication

It is importent that anyone suffering from any type of epilepsy to take their Medication every day at the perscriped times. The Medication you may be perscriped as not takeing the medication or missing a single dose may have dangerouse consiquences. This may include: Having a seizure, loss of balance, deppresion or anxiaty or having a full seizure regaurdless of your conditon.

As it is incredibly importent to take your medicaton you should implement a system to remind your self that you have taken or need to take your meds. The most simple and easy to do is to have a date sheet where you mark off the days you have taken your medicaton. This will allow you to see what days you have missed a dose and when you may need to pick up or pick up your perscriped medication.

Hving epilepsy is not easy and it will require you to make changes to your life. First in the UK you will classed as dissabled, you will also be unable to drive which will serously hinder your ability to get to work or school. However you will be issued a freedom pass by your local county wich gives you free public transport.
